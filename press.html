Bloom Press Kit

Self-care. No pressure. Just you, showing up.

What is Bloom?

Bloom is a free mental health self-care app. A gentle place to track habits without pressure, journal, practice breathing exercises, and show up for yourself at your own pace.

Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no data collection, no ads, no cost. Built from lived experience. Free forever.

Bloom exists because mental health tools should be free, private, and gentle. Built by someone who's been through it, for people going through it. If this app helps even one person feel a little less alone, it was worth building.

Features

  • Habit tracking without consecutive streaks (your total never goes down)
  • Hard day mode (reduces to your top 2 habits, no pressure)
  • Journaling with gentle AI reflections (warm, never clinical advice)
  • Guided breathing exercises
  • Anonymous buddy system for peer encouragement
  • Community encouragement wall
  • Crisis resources always one tap away (988, Crisis Text Line, international)
  • 6 color themes including Pride theme
  • Accessible (screen readers, keyboard navigation, high contrast mode)
  • Works on any device, no download required (but installs as an app too)
  • Every feature is grounded in peer-reviewed research (citations in app under Settings > Science behind Bloom)

Privacy & Safety

Built for young people

Three-tier age gate: under 13 cannot use Bloom, 13 to 15 get solo mode only (no community features), 16+ get full access. No accounts or sign-up required.

Automated content moderation

Every community message is screened before delivery. Grooming, contact exchange, directed harm, and credible threats are all blocked. Self-harm language is never blocked. It surfaces crisis resources instead.

Privacy by default

All personal data (journals, moods, habits) stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics platforms, no ads. Community data is deletable at any time.

Crisis resources always accessible

988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline, Crisis Text Line (741741), and international resources via findahelpline.com. Always one tap away from any screen. Auto-surfaced for low mood and self-harm language.

Usage Guidelines

  • Use the logo as-is. Don't stretch, recolor, or add effects.
  • Keep clear space around the logo (at least half the logo's width).
  • On dark backgrounds, use the logo directly. On light backgrounds, it works fine too (the dark rounded square provides its own contrast).
  • When writing about Bloom, please mention that it's free and open source.
  • Bloom is open source (AGPL-3.0). Anyone can use, modify, and share it, but derivatives must stay open source too.
