Bloom

Self-care. No pressure.

Bloom is a free mental health self-care app. A gentle place to track habits without pressure, journal, practice breathing exercises, and show up for yourself at your own pace. It runs in any browser, no download required. No account, no sign-up. Open the link and start. Everything stays on your device.

What it offers

Mood check-insName how you're feeling. Helps regulate emotions.
Breathing exercisesGuided 4-7-8 technique to calm your nervous system.
JournalingPrompted writing with gentle AI reflections.
Hard day modeOverwhelmed? Reduces to your top 2 habits.
Habit trackingYour total never goes down. Missing a day is fine.
Bloom BuddyAnonymous peer encouragement. No judgment.
Small winsNotice what went right. Counteracts negativity bias.
Crisis resources988, Crisis Text Line, international. Always one tap.

Every feature is grounded in peer-reviewed research. Full citations available in app under Settings > Science behind Bloom.

Privacy

  • All data stays on the user's device. Never uploaded.
  • No accounts, no tracking, no analytics, no ads
  • Users can export or delete all data at any time
  • No protected health information (PHI) collected

Who it's for

  • Anyone building basic self-care habits
  • People managing stress, anxiety, or depression
  • Those who can't access or afford therapy yet
  • Anyone having a hard day who needs a quiet place
Bloom exists because mental health tools should be free, private, and gentle. Built by someone who's been through it, for people going through it. If this app helps even one person feel a little less alone, it was worth building.
