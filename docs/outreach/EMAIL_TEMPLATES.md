>
> Bloom was built by someone who's been through it, for people going through it. No company, no investors, no monetization — just a quiet place to show up for yourself on hard days.

## Tips

- **Keep it short.** Counseling center staff get hundreds of emails. Three short paragraphs beats a wall of text.
- **Lead with "free" and "no data collection."** These are the two things that make institutional decision-makers relax.
- **Ask for feedback, not commitment.** "Would you look at this?" is easier to say yes to than "Would you pilot this?"
- **Follow up once.** If no response after 7-10 days, one polite follow-up. Then move on.
- **Start small.** Community colleges and smaller state universities are more responsive than large research universities. One pilot at a small school is worth more than silence from ten big ones.
- **Name the research.** Institutions trust evidence. Mentioning specific studies (even briefly) signals this isn't just another wellness app.
