What is bloom buddy?

bloom buddy pairs you with someone for mutual encouragement and accountability. Here's what your buddy can see:

Your current mood (Low / Rough / Okay / Good / Great)
Your days shown up (total days active)
Your habit completion % (not which specific habits)
Your display name (set in Settings)

Your buddy cannot see: your journal entries, specific habits, affirmations, or any private data.

You can send each other short messages and quick nudges. If mood sharing is on, your buddy gets a notification on rough days so they can send encouragement. You can turn mood sharing off in Settings at any time.

You can have multiple buddies and unpair at any time.

Why this works

Research shows that simply knowing someone is aware of your efforts is one of the strongest predictors of sustained behavior change.
