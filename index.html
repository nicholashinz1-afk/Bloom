Add bloom to your
home screen

For the best experience — and so you can find it easily — add bloom to your home screen. It takes 10 seconds.

On iPhone (Safari)
1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari ⎙
2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
3. Tap Add — done!
On Android (Chrome)
1. Tap the ⋮ menu in the top right
2. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
3. Confirm — done!
🌱
