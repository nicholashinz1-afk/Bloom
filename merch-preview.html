stay.

One word. One syllable. A period. It means something different to everyone who reads it.

  • For someone in crisis: it's the most important word there is
  • For someone having a hard day: stay present, stay here, don't check out
  • For someone who keeps trying to be different: stay as you are
  • For anyone reading it on a stranger's shirt: it makes you pause and fill in your own meaning

The person wearing it knows what it means to them. The person reading it projects their own. It doesn't explain itself. It doesn't need to.

This is the first piece from a standalone apparel line. No visible brand on the product. Just the word. 100% of profits fund free mental health tools.
