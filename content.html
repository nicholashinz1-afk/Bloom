Mar 31 Feature — No Streak Pressure

Your Streak Didn't Break

Caption
Most habit apps punish you for missing a day. Bloom doesn't. Your total only goes up. Miss a day, miss a week, it doesn't matter. Your progress is still there. Research shows that missing one day has no measurable impact on long-term habit formation (Lally et al., 2010). What matters is showing up over time, not showing up perfectly. That's the whole point. No guilt. No shame. Just you, showing up when you can.

Apr 6 Science — Affect Labeling

Naming It Changes It

Caption
Naming how you feel actually changes what's happening in your brain. It's called affect labeling. When you put words to your emotions, activity in the amygdala (your brain's alarm system) goes down. You're not just venting. You're regulating. That's why Bloom starts with a mood check-in every day. Not to judge how you're feeling. Just to name it. Lieberman et al., 2007. Putting Feelings into Words.

Apr 8 Feature — Breathing

4-7-8

Caption
4 seconds in. 7 seconds hold. 8 seconds out. The 4-7-8 breathing technique activates your parasympathetic nervous system. That's the part of you that calms down, slows your heart rate, and tells your body it's safe. It takes less than 2 minutes. Bloom walks you through it. Next time you feel your chest tighten or your thoughts start racing, try it. It's free and it's in the app.
