# SaidGently — Brand & Design Guide

## Identity

- **Shop name:** SaidGently
- **Etsy:** etsy.com/shop/SaidGently
- **Shop title:** One word at a time.
- **Tagline:** 100% of profits fund free mental health tools.
- **Relationship to Bloom:** SaidGently is a separate entity that funds Bloom. Bloom's name does not appear on any product. The connection is revealed through the Etsy buyer message and packaging inserts (when added). On the founder's personal social media, the connection is stated openly.

## Philosophy

- **No visible brand on the product.** The phrases are the product. No logos, no labels competing with the words.
- **One phrase at a time.** Each drop centers on a single word or phrase. The first is "stay."
- **Two energies:** Quiet pieces (tee, heavyweight hoodie, hats) and loud pieces (sun hoodies). Same word, different volume.
- **Quality blanks.** Premium garments that people want to wear regardless of the text. Comfort Colors, Cotton Heritage, Flexfit, Richardson.
- **Earthy, grounded palette.** Forest greens, olive, loden, rust, charcoal. Amber/old gold text and embroidery throughout.

## The Phrase: "Stay."

One word. One syllable. A period. This is the hook.

- **For someone in crisis:** it's the most important word there is
- **For someone having a hard day:** stay present, stay here, don't check out
- **For someone who keeps trying to be different:** stay as you are
- **For anyone reading it on a stranger's shirt:** it makes you pause and fill in your own meaning

It doesn't explain itself. It doesn't need to. The person wearing it knows what it means to them, and the person reading it projects their own meaning onto it.

## Typography

- **Apparel text:** Fraunces, weight 400 (Regular), lowercase with period: `stay.`
- **Hat embroidery:** Fraunces, weight 500 (Medium) for thread legibility
- **Color:** Soft amber (#d4ad6a) for DTG print, Old Gold (Printful thread #1672) for embroidery
- **Hat embroidery type:** Flat embroidery only. No 3D puff (distorts thin serif strokes and the period).
