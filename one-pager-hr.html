Bloom

Self-care. No pressure.
For Workplace Wellness Programs

Bloom is a free mental health self-care app your employees can use right now, with no setup, no licensing, and no data liability. It runs in any browser and helps people track habits, journal, practice breathing exercises, and take care of themselves at their own pace. Share it in a wellness newsletter, add it to your benefits page, or include it in mental health awareness materials. That's it.

Why HR teams like Bloom

Zero cost
No licensing, no contracts, no per-seat pricing. Free forever.
Zero IT
Runs in any browser. Nothing to provision or maintain.
Zero data liability
All data stays on-device. No PHI, no PII collected.
Zero friction
No accounts, no sign-up, no app download. Just a link.

How organizations use it

  • Include in mental health awareness month materials
  • Add to employee wellness resource pages or intranet
  • Share in wellness newsletters or all-hands emails
  • Recommend alongside EAP as a daily self-care companion
  • Offer to employees on therapy waitlists
  • Feature in onboarding packets or benefits guides
